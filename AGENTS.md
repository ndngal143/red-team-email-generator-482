## Project
- Title: Red Team Social Engineering Email Generator (For Authorized)
- Scenario: Red teams need to generate highly customized phishing emails for internal security awareness exercises.
- Approach: Users provide the target profile (e.g., “finance employee”) and contextual details (e.g., “recent reimbursement policy change”). The LLM generates a persuasive phishing email draft with appropriate language, urgency, and lure links, while performing anti-spam detection checks to avoid common spam indicators.
- Deliverable: A web-based form generator with adjustable deception intensity and tone (urgent, professional, friendly).
- Advanced Component: Integrate a link generator that creates realistic internal-looking URLs and maintain generated email logs for defensive model training.
